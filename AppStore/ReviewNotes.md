# App Review Notes

MathFeedback is a local-first productivity app for high-school math teachers and tutors.

- The app does not require login.
- The app does not connect to a developer server.
- The app does not use analytics, ads, tracking, or third-party SDKs.
- All student and feedback data is stored locally with SwiftData.
- Data export/import is user-initiated through JSON backup files.
